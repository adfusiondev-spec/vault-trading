Introduction

What is NOKHBA?

NOKHBA is a white-label, multi-tenant B2B SaaS trading platform built for brokers and financial institutions. As a Sub-Admin (tenant operator), you have your own isolated workspace — your own traders, CRM pipeline, sales team, and financial desk — completely separate from every other broker on the platform.

Role Overview

Sub-Admin Tenant Operator

Full access to your company's CRM dashboard. Manage traders, process deposits & withdrawals, monitor all live trades, run the sales team, and manage your subscription and payment gateways.

Dashboard URL: /sub-admin/[your-slug]
Sales Sales Member

Scoped view: only your assigned leads and traders. Monitor trades from your assigned clients, advance leads through the 9-stage funnel, and convert qualified leads into live trader accounts.

Client Management

2.1 Inviting New Traders

Traders register via a unique invite link tied to your account. NOKHBA does not allow open public registration — every trader must be linked to a broker at sign-up.

How to generate and share an invite link
1 Open the Clients tab in your CRM dashboard.
2 Locate the Trader Invite Link panel near the top of the page.
3 Click Copy to copy your unique invite URL to the clipboard.
4 Share the URL with your client. When they register through that link, they are automatically linked to your account.

Tip: Sales team members can also invite traders using their personal referral_code. When a trader registers via a referral link, they are linked to both the sales rep and your brokerage.

2.2 Adding a Client Directly

Sub-Admins can register a trader directly from the dashboard without sending an invite link.

Steps — Add Client modal
1 Click Add Client in the Clients tab.
2 Fill in: Full Name, Email, Password, Phone, Country.
3 Click Create. The account is active immediately — no email verification required for directly created accounts.

2.3 Client Detail Page

Click Manage → on any client row to open their full detail page. This page is split into two panels:

Left Panel — Trading Terminal
  • 📊 Quick stats: Total Deposits, Total Withdrawals, Closed P&L, Open Positions
  • ⚡ Execute trades on behalf of the client (asset selector, BUY/SELL)
  • 📋 Live open positions table with Emergency Close button per trade
  • 💳 Full transaction history (deposits & withdrawals)
  • 💰 Balance adjustment — credit or debit the wallet with a note
Right Panel — CRM & Security
  • 🏷️ Lead status dropdown (9 stages)
  • ✏️ Edit client info: Name, Phone, Country
  • 🔐 Reveal / reset client password
  • 🚫 Ban / Unban client (blocks login)
  • 📝 Retention & call notes (free text, saved to profile)

2.4 Leads & CRM Pipeline

The Leads tab gives you a full pipeline view of all prospective traders. Leads can be created manually, imported in bulk (CSV), or assigned to specific sales reps.

9 Lead Status Stages

New Prospect Hot Lead Active In Negotiation Contacted Prospect Cold Inactive Active / Funded

Update a lead's status using the inline dropdown on any leads row. Status updates are saved instantly — no extra save button required.

2.5 Converting a Lead to a Trader

When a lead is ready to open a live trading account, convert them directly from the Leads tab. This creates a full Supabase Auth account and links them to your brokerage in one step.

Lead Conversion Flow
1 Find the lead in the Leads tab and click Convert →.
2 A modal appears showing the lead's name and email. Enter an initial password (minimum 8 characters).
3 Click ✓ Convert. A trader account is created. The lead status automatically updates to Active / Funded.
4 Share the login credentials with the trader. They can log in immediately at /login and change their password from their profile.

Note: Trader accounts start as inactive pending review. A Sub-Admin must activate the account from the Clients tab before the trader can execute live orders.

2.6 Managing Your Sales Team

The Sales Team tab lets you create and manage sales rep accounts within your tenant. Each sales rep gets their own login and a scoped view of only their assigned leads and clients. The number of sales reps you can add is determined by your subscription plan's sales seat limit.

Action How
Create a sales rep Click Add Sales Member, fill in name, email, password
Assign a lead In the Leads tab, set the Assigned To field on any lead row
Assign a trader On the client detail page, use the Sales Rep assignment field
Remove a sales rep Delete from the Sales Team tab — their leads/clients become unassigned

Trade Monitor

The Trade Monitor tab gives you a real-time feed of every active trade across all your clients. Data updates automatically via Supabase Realtime — no page refresh needed.

3.1 Understanding the Trade Table

Column Description
Trader Full name of the client who placed the trade
Asset Trading symbol (e.g. BTCUSDT, XAUUSD, EUR/USD)
Type BUY or SELL direction
Amount USD value of the position at entry
P&L Unrealised profit/loss at current market price — green when profitable, red when at a loss
Status Open / Closed
Details DETAILS button — navigates to that client's full detail page

3.2 Emergency Close

From a client's detail page (accessible via the DETAILS button on any trade row), Sub-Admins can force-close any open position on behalf of a trader using the EMERGENCY CLOSE button on each open position row.

Caution: Emergency close executes at the current market price immediately. This action cannot be undone. Always confirm with the client before closing on their behalf, except in genuine emergency situations (account protection, compliance requirement).

3.3 Sales Team — Trade View

Sales reps see the same Trade Monitor layout but only see trades from traders assigned to them. The Emergency Close action is not available to the Sales role — trade execution actions are restricted to Sub-Admins only.

Financial Operations

The Deposits and Withdrawals tabs form your Financial Desk. All client funding requests arrive here as Pending items. You review, verify, and approve or reject them. Database triggers handle all balance adjustments automatically — no manual editing needed.

4.1 Processing Deposits

Column Description
Date Submission timestamp
Client Trader's full name (links to their detail page)
Amount USD amount requested
Method USDT TRC20, Bitcoin, or Bank Transfer
Status Pending / Approved / Rejected
Detail Opens the full transaction detail modal
Approving a Deposit
1 Click DETAIL on any pending deposit row.
2 The detail modal shows: amount, payment method, reference number, and the payment proof image or PDF (rendered inline).
3 Verify the proof matches the declared amount and your payment gateway records.
4 Click Approve. The client's wallet balance is credited immediately via a database trigger. A real-time notification is sent to the trader.
✕ If the proof is invalid, click Reject instead. The balance is not touched; the trader is notified of the rejection.

4.2 Processing Withdrawals

Withdrawal requests include the trader's destination details. NOKHBA displays sensitive wallet addresses and IBANs in a secure truncated format in the table and the full details in the modal.

Destination Type What you see
Crypto wallet First 10 + last 6 characters of the address (monospace)
Bank transfer Bank Name, Account Holder, masked IBAN

Approval flow: Same as deposits — open DETAIL modal, verify destination, click Approve. On approval, the database trigger deducts the amount from the trader's balance. On rejection, no balance change occurs.

4.3 Manual Balance Adjustment

Sub-Admins can credit or debit a client's wallet directly from the Client Detail page — useful for corrections, bonuses, or removing erroneous amounts. Balance adjustments are visible only to Sub-Admins, not to sales reps.

How to adjust a balance
  1. Navigate to the client's detail page (Clients tab → Manage →).
  2. Locate the Balance Adjustment widget in the right CRM panel.
  3. Enter a positive number (credit) or a negative number (debit) and add an optional note.
  4. Click Apply. The wallet balance updates immediately.

4.4 Your Subscription Payments

The Subscription tab shows your full payment history with NOKHBA and lets you submit new subscription payments.

Submitting a subscription payment

3 Choose a Payment Method. Your gateway addresses appear automatically with copy buttons.
4 Upload your Payment Proof (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or PDF).
5 Click Submit. The NOKHBA master admin reviews your submission and activates or upgrades your plan.

Payment Settings

The Payment Settings tab lets you configure the wallet addresses and bank details that your traders see when making deposits. You can override the platform's global defaults with your own addresses for each payment method.

5.1 Available Payment Methods

USDT (Tether)
  • Wallet Address
  • Network: TRC20 / ERC20 / BEP20
  • Active toggle
Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Wallet Address (Legacy or SegWit)
  • Active toggle
Bank Transfer
  • Bank Name
  • Account Holder
  • RIB / IBAN
  • Active toggle

5.2 How to Configure Gateways

1 Open the Payment Settings tab from your CRM sidebar.
2 For each method you want to activate, toggle the Active switch to on.
3 Fill in your wallet address or bank details in the provided fields.
4 Click Save Payment Settings. A ✓ Saved Successfully confirmation appears.
5 Your traders will see your configured addresses the next time they initiate a deposit — no restart or re-login required.

How addresses are inherited: If you have not configured a specific method, your traders will see the platform-level default addresses set by the NOKHBA master admin. Your settings always override the defaults when present.

5.3 Trial Plan Restriction

During the 1-Day Free Trial, the Payment Settings tab is visible but all input fields are disabled. An information banner reads:

"Option not allowed in the Trial Plan."

To unlock Payment Settings configuration, submit a subscription payment to upgrade your plan from Trial to Standard or VIP.

5.4 Security & Best Practices

Double-check wallet addresses: Payment proof images cannot prove a transaction went to your address if the displayed address is wrong. Always verify your saved address after entering it.

Disable unused methods: If you only accept USDT, toggle Bitcoin and Bank Transfer to inactive. This prevents traders from selecting a method you don't support.

Update promptly on address changes: If your wallet address changes (e.g. exchange migration), update it here immediately and inform any traders with in-flight transactions.

5.5 Client Password Security

NOKHBA stores trader passwords using AES-256-CBC encryption with a random IV. Sub-Admins can reveal a client's current password from the Client Detail page using the Password field with the eye icon — useful for onboarding support without requiring a password reset flow.

Action How
Reveal current password Click the 👁 eye icon on the Password field
Copy password to clipboard Click the copy icon next to the revealed password
Set a new password Enter a new value in the New Password field and click Reset Password
